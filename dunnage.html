Dunnage Facilitation

Dunnage refers to materials used to secure and protect cargo during transportation. It plays a crucial role in shipping and logistics by preventing damage to goods and ensuring they reach their destination in optimal condition.

Our dunnage materials meet stringent quality standards and comply with industry regulations for safe transportation.

Types of Dunnage:

Wood: Traditional and sturdy, ideal for heavy or irregularly shaped items.

Plastic: Lightweight and versatile, suitable for various types of cargo.

Provides cushioning and shock absorption, perfect for delicate goods.

Inflatable bags that fill void spaces, offering dynamic support and protection.

Benefits of Dunnage:

  • Protects goods from damage during transit.
  • Prevents shifting and sliding of cargo.
  • Ensures compliance with shipping regulations regarding cargo stabilization.

    • Customization Options:

    Dunnage solutions can be tailored to specific needs, including:

  • Size: Custom sizes to fit various cargo dimensions.
  • Shape: Molded to accommodate unique shapes or configurations.
  • Materials: Selection of materials based on cargo type and transportation conditions.
